# Job Market & Unemployment Analysis 📊

## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of job market trends and unemployment patterns across major U.S. metropolitan areas. The analysis combines economic, demographic, educational, and skill-based indicators to uncover meaningful insights into labor market conditions.

The project investigates how unemployment rates change over time, how job availability varies across locations, the influence of education and age groups on employment, and how skill diversity impacts job opportunities.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze unemployment trends over time
* Identify seasonal and quarter-wise employment patterns
* Compare unemployment rates across different locations
* Examine the relationship between unemployment rate and job postings
* Study the impact of education level on unemployment
* Analyze unemployment across different age groups
* Explore how skill diversity influences job availability
* Perform statistical analysis and feature engineering on job market data

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains detailed job market records from various U.S. metropolitan areas, including:
